MacroKey Documentation
====================================

**Commands can be a pain to type, and even more of a pain to remember.** 

What if there was a mod that makes your life easier by allowing you to connect all those critical commands to a single button press. When you find yourself in a situation where you want to be able to access a command fast, you could then be 1 click away from achieving your goal.
